It supplies 30 m acquisition-based burn probability and classification across forest, shrub, and grass ecosystems through a live official route. It can challenge both burned and background candidates without inheriting the Sentinel single-tile constraint, but it remains algorithmic reference evidence. The public STAC metadata is live while the advertised asset probe redirects to EROS authentication, so acquisition remains gated rather than assumed.

The accepted seven-bundle request remains pending under issue #416; this scout neither duplicates it nor substitutes for delivery.
