Decision guide

Burned
The center pixel has visible pre/post evidence consistent with a burn scar.
Background
The center pixel has usable evidence supporting no burn scar.
Uncertain
Burn and non-burn interpretations remain plausible or source evidence conflicts.
Unusable evidence
Cloud, smoke, shadow, missing support, registration, or another defect prevents a judgment.
Sufficient
The shown evidence supports the selected label without a material unresolved limitation.
Limited
A label is possible, but an explicit limitation reduces confidence.
Insufficient
The evidence does not support a label; use uncertain or unusable as appropriate.

Blank or uniform source-context panels can mean the sampled center is outside the displayed NIFC or MTBS class. Absence from source context is not proof of background.
