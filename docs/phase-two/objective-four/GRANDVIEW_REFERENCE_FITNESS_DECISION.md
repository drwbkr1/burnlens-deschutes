## Actual evidence

The exact v0.39 Grandview optical package is re-verified before comparison. The optical boundary contains 62,588 native 20 m pixel centers and 61,073 valid pair pixels; all nine registration windows still pass.

Nearest-neighbor sampling from native 30 m claims no resolution gain. The MTBS grid covers 61,795 optical-boundary pixel centers (98.7330%); 793 remain explicitly unresolved. MTBS classes 2-4 occupy 58,438 pixels and 56,943 of those also have valid optical comparison. Seventy MTBS pixels are increased greenness and remain separately reported.

The delivered RAVG record says much of the event has sparse to no pre-fire tree cover. Because RAVG models are calibrated for forest cover, their modeled values are less reliable here. BurnLens renders RAVG CBI, basal-area, and canopy-cover products only as limited context and does not count their agreement as independent affirmative evidence.
