## Credential boundary

Official Earthdata Login and LP DAAC guidance state that DAAC byte downloads require an Earthdata Login account and application authorization. The products' open-use posture and their credentialed delivery posture are separate facts. Adding or using an Earthdata username, password, token, cookie, `.netrc`, or application authorization is an owner stop condition under the controlling goal.
