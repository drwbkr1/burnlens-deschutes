# TERMS-2026-032 - Exact delivered Windigo reference terms

**Unit / issue:** `P2O4-T35-U03` / #534

**Reviewed:** 2026-07-23 UTC

**Decision:** `RESOLVED_FOR_ATTRIBUTED_BOUNDED_PROTOTYPE_EVIDENCE_WITH_PROGRAM_LIMITATIONS`

The exact delivered FGDC and ISO metadata govern reference use in this checkpoint.

- BAER SBS is publicly available. Use requires reasonable and proper acknowledgement and visible treatment as preliminary/draft data. Its BAER-team field-informed provenance belongs to the source; BurnLens must not claim its own field validation.
- MTBS access constraints are `None`. Use has no restriction except reasonable and proper acknowledgement. MTBS remains an analyst-interpreted landscape-scale product with plot-data, geographic-accuracy, update, warranty, and fitness limitations.
- RAVG access constraints are `None`. Use has no restriction except reasonable and proper acknowledgement. RAVG estimates forest-vegetation effects with modeled relationships affected by delayed mortality, resprouting, vegetation domain, and source-location limitations.
- All three programs reserve correction, update, modification, or replacement and disclaim warranty or fitness for other purposes.

Terms pass for deterministic local inspection and publication of BurnLens-derived fitness summaries and rendered comparisons with attribution. Provider raster, vector, PDF, XML, KMZ, recipient, and retrieval-route bytes remain ignored and are not redistributed. Public evidence must acknowledge the USDA Forest Service BAER program, the Monitoring Trends in Burn Severity Project of the USGS and USDA Forest Service, the USDA Forest Service RAVG program, and modified Copernicus Sentinel data.

The allowed role is bounded prototype evidence only:

- BAER SBS classes 2-4 may act as primary field-informed positive-reference evidence;
- MTBS classes 2-4 may corroborate a positive proposal;
- RAVG products may provide modeled vegetation-effect context only; and
- no delivered zero, low, unchanged, nodata, outside-perimeter, or other class is affirmative background truth.

This review does not authorize raw redistribution, independent ground truth, accepted labels, a dataset, a split, a baseline, a model, accuracy, BurnLens field validation, official status, endorsement, operational readiness, emergency suitability, or a fitness-for-purpose claim.
