# TERMS-2026-009 - Current Burn Severity Catalog and Bundle-Acquisition Boundary

**Reviewed:** 2026-07-17 UTC

**Issue:** #411

## Decision

`RESOLVED_FOR_PUBLIC_CATALOG_INVENTORY_AND_PRIVATE_EXACT_BUNDLE_ACQUISITION; BUNDLE_REDISTRIBUTION_AND_NON_USGS_CONTENT_PENDING_INSPECTION`.

USGS states that data authored or produced by USGS are generally public domain, asks for acknowledgment, and warns that some material on USGS sites may be owned by others. The selected portal represents joint federal fire-mapping programs and products, including USGS and USDA Forest Service roles. BurnLens may query the public catalog and privately acquire exact current bundles for inspection while preserving program attribution and avoiding endorsement claims.

This decision does not authorize redistribution of an unopened bundle or any marked third-party content. Bundle-specific attribution, notices, and non-USGS material must be inspected before committing source bytes or publishing derived bundle content. An unresolved restriction is a stop condition.

## Required public wording

Inventory evidence must name the relevant BAER, RAVG, and MTBS programs and identify the U.S. Geological Survey and USDA Forest Service source context. It must say that catalog presence is availability evidence, not field truth, label validation, accuracy evidence, official status, or endorsement.

Any later derived pixel evidence must identify the exact source program and product, disclose BurnLens transformations, preserve required attribution, and distinguish analyst-interpreted remotely sensed reference from ground or field validation.

## Data and access boundary

- Public WFS catalog access uses no credential, secret, paid service, account change, or sharing-state change.
- Exact product bundles may be retained only in ignored local storage until byte, content, and terms inspection passes.
- No provider bundle byte is committed by this checkpoint.
- The property-only WFS response and normalized public inventory may be represented as derived evidence with exact request and hashes.
- No current product is called fit merely because the portal lists it.
