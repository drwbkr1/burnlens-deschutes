# TERMS-2026-019 - Green Ridge extended optical evidence

**Reviewed:** 2026-07-20 UTC

**Issue:** #480

The current Copernicus Data Space Ecosystem terms state that Sentinel data access and use are free, full, and open under the Sentinel Data Legal Notice. BurnLens must retain attribution as `Contains modified Copernicus Sentinel data 2020-2021, accessed through CDSE.` Portal material outside Sentinel data has narrower reuse terms and is not republished. Account credentials, tokens, private download headers, and local credential-store details remain secret and uncommitted.

Current CDSE documentation confirms the `https://stac.dataspace.copernicus.eu/v1/` catalogue route and token-authenticated OData product download. Service availability is not guaranteed, quotas must not be bypassed, and credentials must not be hardcoded. The repository uses one existing authorized account, one exact frozen product contract, bounded retries, and no paid service.

Sentinel-2 L2A surface reflectance and SCL are optical/quality evidence. SCL, catalog cloud percentage, spectral stability, outside-perimeter status, MTBS/RAVG class 0 or 1, and lack of a burn signature cannot independently establish background truth. MTBS remains analyst-interpreted and not comprehensively field validated; RAVG remains a rapid forest-vegetation assessment with timing and applicability limits. The checkpoint may establish only a reproducible background-candidate evidence route for later separate owner yes/no/uncertain review.

No unresolved licensing or terms issue blocks exact acquisition under this bounded role. Any later change in source, paid service, redistribution posture, access status, or attribution must trigger a fresh terms review.
