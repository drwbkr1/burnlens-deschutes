# TERMS-2026-018 - Green Ridge Delivered Reference Terms

**Date:** 2026-07-19  
**Issue / branch:** #477 / `codex/p2o4-t22-green-ridge-reference-fitness`

The exact delivered FGDC and ISO metadata govern this checkpoint.

- **BAER:** thresholded preliminary BARC4/BARC256 products are delivered only to BAER teams and further distribution is at the team leader's discretion. They are absent from this archive. The present Landsat reflectance and unthresholded continuous products may be used with reasonable source acknowledgement and explicit preliminary/draft limitations. BurnLens publishes no provider pixel bytes and treats the products as continuous context only.
- **MTBS:** access constraints are `None`; use requires reasonable and proper acknowledgement. Credit the Monitoring Trends in Burn Severity Project, U.S. Geological Survey, and USDA Forest Service. Analyst interpretation, suitability, update, location, and no-warranty limits remain visible.
- **RAVG:** access constraints are `None`; use requires reasonable and proper acknowledgement. Credit the USDA Forest Service and ESA with `Contains modified Copernicus Sentinel data 2020`. RAVG is designed for forested lands and modeled vegetation effects; timing, non-tree vegetation, delayed mortality, resprouting, cloud, and model-fit limits remain binding.
- **Optical evidence:** retain `Contains modified Copernicus Sentinel data 2020, accessed through CDSE` for the separately registered Sentinel pair.

Terms are resolved for bounded inspection, derived visualization, and owner-review evidence in this checkpoint. They do not authorize publishing provider raster bytes, restricted BAER classified products, or claims of independent ground truth, field validation, official status, endorsement, suitability, or operational readiness. If a later checkpoint proposes redistribution of provider pixels or a use outside these roles, terms must be reviewed again.
