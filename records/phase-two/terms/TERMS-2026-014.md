The live product descriptions still distinguish pre/post imagery, continuous indices, thematic severity, perimeters, and explicit anomaly masks. MTBS remains contextual reference evidence and cannot become BurnLens ground truth, official validation, or a universal threshold.

The exact current reference bundle restrictions remain binding; restricted thresholded Tepee BARC stays private and excluded.
