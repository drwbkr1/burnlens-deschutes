# TERMS-2026-012 - Delivered BAER/RAVG/MTBS Product Terms

**Reviewed:** 2026-07-17 UTC

**Issue:** #416

## Decision

`RESOLVED_FOR_BOUNDED_AGGREGATE_AND_RENDERED_OWNER_REVIEW_EVIDENCE_WITH_THRESHOLD_BARC_EXCLUSION`.

MTBS and RAVG metadata state no use restriction beyond reasonable acknowledgement and retain warranty, positional-accuracy, and update cautions. USGS works are generally public domain; attribution remains required and endorsement must not be implied.

The delivered BAER metadata is stricter. Thresholded preliminary BARC4/BARC256 is delivered to BAER teams and further distribution is at the BAER team leader's discretion. Unthresholded pre/post reflectance and dNBR may be public, subject to acknowledgement and preliminary/draft limitations. BurnLens therefore:

- retains exact Tepee BARC4/BARC256 bytes only in ignored/private storage;
- does not include their pixels, class counts, derived maps, or thresholds in public evidence;
- does not use them to promote a label;
- may summarize the separately public unthresholded Tepee dNBR as continuous change context; and
- records that the Darlene delivery contains SBS metadata but no delivered classified SBS raster.

No unresolved licensing or terms condition remains for this checkpoint because restricted thresholded BAER pixels are excluded. A later public surface must preserve these same restrictions and source acknowledgements.
