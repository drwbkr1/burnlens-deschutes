**Reviewed:** 2026-07-17 UTC

## Decision

`RESOLVED_FOR_PUBLIC_METADATA_RECONNAISSANCE; EXACT_PRODUCT_RETENTION_AND_DERIVED_PIXEL_PUBLICATION_REQUIRE_PRODUCT_LEVEL_REVIEW`.

The scout may retain and publish normalized official metadata, official routes, public identifiers, response hashes, rankings, and limitations. It may not treat that permission as authorization to redistribute an exact provider bundle, marked third-party content, authenticated asset, or derived provider pixel.

## Source-specific result

- USGS catalog and Landsat metadata are usable with citation; Landsat states no use restrictions. Exact authenticated asset access remains unresolved because the advertised route redirected to EROS login.
- Sentinel-2 remains governed by the Copernicus Sentinel legal notice and attribution requirements.
- NASA-led products default to CC0 unless a specific product is marked otherwise; attribution is still required and endorsement must not be implied.
- Annual NLCD Collection 1.2 is CC0 1.0 with citation.
- Burn Severity Portal catalog reconnaissance is resolved; every delivered BAER/RAVG/MTBS archive notice and marked non-USGS component must still be inspected before retention or publication.
- NIFC WFIGS is public official context; its disclaimer and attribution remain binding.

## Binding acquisition gate

Before retaining any new source pixels, verify exact identity, terms/notices, full-boundary coverage, CRS, grid, nodata, QA, time relation, and program-specific limitations. Stop on an unresolved license, terms condition, or third-party-content notice. Do not bypass provider access controls or infer that a login redirect is downloadable data.
