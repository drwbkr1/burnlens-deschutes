# TERMS-2026-021 - Grandview Official Reference Request

**Reviewed:** 2026-07-20 UTC

**Issue:** #499

The official Burn Severity Portal presents BAER, MTBS, and RAVG products for public data access through the consolidated viewer and direct-download routes. The bounded queue request is free, uses the owner's existing recipient address, adds no account, paid service, or secret, and requests only the three frozen standard maps in native UTM. Queue capacity must be respected; an unknown POST outcome must not be retried automatically.

Public program pages establish role limits before delivery. BAER BARC is preliminary support for later team assessment and may not be publicly available. MTBS is a consistently derived landscape-scale program with assessment timing chosen by ecosystem and analyst interpretation. RAVG estimates post-fire vegetation condition primarily for forested National Forest System lands; the exact Grandview catalog warning makes sparse/non-tree cover a binding reliability limitation. None is independent ground truth, and no class or boundary can become a BurnLens label automatically.

The 2026 first-quarter release notice states that the portal archive was reprocessed with contemporary imagery/calibration and native WGS84 UTM production. Legacy and current products can differ at individual pixels and in mapped acreage even when the boundary is unchanged. The exact delivered archive notices and metadata will govern later inspection. BurnLens must stop if they expose an unresolved access, redistribution, or use restriction; it will not publish provider raster bytes.

Terms are resolved for one exact no-cost request and private custody of a resulting delivery. They are not yet sufficient for pixel use or derived public visualization; those decisions require inspection of the delivered terms, identities, products, and program-specific notices before any pixel is used.
