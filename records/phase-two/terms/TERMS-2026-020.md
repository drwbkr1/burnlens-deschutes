# TERMS-2026-020 — Grandview Copernicus Sentinel Use

**Issue:** #495

**Reviewed:** 2026-07-20

**Primary sources:** [CDSE Terms and Conditions](https://dataspace.copernicus.eu/terms-and-conditions), [Sentinel Data Legal Notice](https://sentinels.copernicus.eu/documents/247904/690755/Sentinel_Data_Legal_Notice), and [official CDSE OData documentation](https://documentation.dataspace.copernicus.eu/APIs/OData.html).

Current CDSE terms continue to make Copernicus Sentinel data available on a free, full, and open basis under the Sentinel Data Legal Notice. Lawful reproduction, distribution, communication, adaptation, modification, and combination are allowed without warranty of quality, suitability, or service availability. Public derived evidence must identify the source as `Contains modified Copernicus Sentinel data 2021, accessed through CDSE.`

Whole-product OData download requires an authorized token. BurnLens uses one existing owner-authorized account, the documented HTTPS identity/token/download hosts, an exact two-product contract, bounded retries, and ignored repository-local custody. It does not hardcode or retain credentials, tokens, headers, private account data, or signed redirects. It does not bypass quotas or add a paid service.

**Decision:** Terms and attribution are resolved for exact Grandview Sentinel acquisition and experimental derived evidence. The grant does not make BurnLens official, endorsed, field-validated, accurate, operational, emergency-ready, or fit for any purpose.
