# TERMS-2026-016 - Green Ridge Sentinel Acquisition

**Issue:** #472
**Reviewed:** 2026-07-19

The current CDSE terms state that Copernicus Sentinel data access and use are free, full, and open under the Sentinel Data Legal Notice. The separate restrictions for other portal content do not replace the Sentinel-data grant. Current official token and OData documentation require an authorized token for whole-product download and warn against hardcoding credentials.

BurnLens may acquire the two exact Green Ridge Sentinel-2 L2A products into ignored repository-local custody, inspect native product structure and pixels, and publish bounded modified evidence. Public derivatives must use the applicable `Contains modified Copernicus Sentinel data [year]` notice, recognize source roles, preserve no-warranty and no-suitability posture, and imply no Copernicus, EU, ESA, CDSE, USGS, USDA Forest Service, or incident endorsement.

The action uses the existing free owner-authorized CDSE account. It adds no paid service, secret, ownership/access change, or redistribution of protected portal material. Native archives will not be committed or published.

Primary terms and operational sources:

- https://dataspace.copernicus.eu/terms-and-conditions
- https://documentation.dataspace.copernicus.eu/APIs/Token.html
- https://documentation.dataspace.copernicus.eu/APIs/OData.html
- https://documentation.dataspace.copernicus.eu/Quotas.html

**Decision:** `RESOLVED_FOR_TWO_EXACT_GREEN_RIDGE_SENTINEL_ARCHIVES_AND_BOUNDED_MODIFIED_EVIDENCE`.
