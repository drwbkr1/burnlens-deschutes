# TERMS-2026-003 - NIFC Reference Geometry Use Review

**Checked:** 2026-07-13 local / 2026-07-14 UTC

**Decision:** Resolved for the exact public research snapshot and derived AOI evidence

## Exact-use finding

NIFC's official maps page directs users to Wildland Fire Open Data for historical fire perimeters and describes the data as downloadable for research or projects and ready for mapping software. The exact WFIGS item is public, is owned by `NIFC_Authoritative`, exposes GeoJSON through a public service, and provides credits plus a data-use disclaimer rather than a redistribution or account restriction.

Those current terms resolve access, repository retention, checksum registration, derivation, and evidence rendering for the exact feature snapshot used by issue #321. This decision does not create a general license conclusion for every NIFC product or future item revision.

- Do not represent the geometry as current incident information or as a BurnLens detection/label.

## Public-domain context

The federal data catalog describes the related DOI/BLM fire-perimeter publication as public, intended for public access and use, and labeled `us-pd`, while directing post-2019 users to the NIFC WFIGS history service. BurnLens does not rely on that related layer as the source geometry; it is supporting federal publication context. The exact WFIGS item's own public access, research/project download purpose, credits, and disclaimer control this checkpoint.
