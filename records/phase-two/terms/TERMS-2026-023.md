# TERMS-2026-023 - Grandview extended Sentinel use

**Issue:** #503

**Reviewed:** 2026-07-20

**Primary sources:** [CDSE Terms and Conditions](https://dataspace.copernicus.eu/terms-and-conditions), [Sentinel Data Legal Notice](https://sentinels.copernicus.eu/documents/247904/690755/Sentinel_Data_Legal_Notice), and [official CDSE OData documentation](https://documentation.dataspace.copernicus.eu/APIs/OData.html).

Current CDSE terms continue to make Copernicus Sentinel data available on a free, full, and open basis under the Sentinel Data Legal Notice. Public derived evidence must identify the source as `Contains modified Copernicus Sentinel data 2021-2022, accessed through CDSE.` No warranty of quality, suitability, or service availability is inferred.

Whole-product OData download requires an authorized token. BurnLens may use the existing owner-authorized account only through the documented HTTPS identity/token/download hosts, an exact one-product contract, bounded retries, and ignored repository-local custody. Credentials, tokens, headers, private account data, and signed redirects must not be retained or printed. No quota bypass or paid service is authorized.

**Decision:** Terms and attribution are resolved for exact experimental acquisition and derived evidence. This grant does not make BurnLens official, endorsed, field-validated, accurate, operational, emergency-ready, or fit for any purpose.
