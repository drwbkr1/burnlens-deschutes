# SOURCE_PRECEDENCE-2026-007 - Current Reference Freshness

## Decision

For any new label-evidence decision, current exact official product bundles outrank a frozen legacy service extract for source freshness. That precedence does not make BAER, RAVG, or MTBS ground truth and does not let a thematic class automatically create a BurnLens label.

Precedence for this remediation is:

1. official use boundaries and explicit no-go claims;
2. the frozen event identities and exact current portal product identities in `SOURCE-2026-014`;
3. exact current BAER/RAVG/MTBS bundle bytes after terms and structural verification;
4. exact Sentinel optical-pair evidence and proposal trace;
5. the one exact blinded reviewer response under the disclosed owner waiver;
6. `SOURCE-2026-013` as frozen historical proposal provenance; and
7. heuristic threshold behavior.

Conflicts are preserved and explained. Cross-program disagreement increases uncertainty; it is never averaged into confidence. Official sources govern official incident facts, while BurnLens remains an experimental portfolio analysis.
