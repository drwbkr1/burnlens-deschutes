# USE-BOUNDARY-2026-026 - Region-Candidate Pilot

## Permitted

- Use the 24 exact approved centers as private seeds for deterministic candidate construction.
- Publish aggregate candidate metadata, actual optical/reference evidence, and private-safe full-grid rasters with 0 outside, 1 core, and 2 unknown ring.
- Describe a candidate as proposed burned or proposed background while preserving its non-label status.
- Build a later separately versioned yes/no/uncertain owner-review surface after this pilot ships.

## Prohibited

- Treat a candidate core as a label, mask truth, official perimeter, or accepted training area.
- Clip or expand a component to meet the one-hectare selection target, or convert ring/outside pixels into background.
- Publish private seed identities, coordinates, owner point decisions, response paths, or restricted provider content.
- Infer region decisions from prior point responses or claim independent review, inter-rater agreement, field validation, official status, endorsement, or operational readiness.
- Create a dataset, split, baseline, model, metric, or Phase Three readiness decision in this checkpoint.
