## Roles and limitations

- MTBS event identities and boundaries govern the event/group contract, but MTBS remains revisable analyst-interpreted remote-sensing reference evidence rather than field truth or an incident perimeter.
- BAER and RAVG catalog presence provides independently traceable program context; it does not guarantee exact product fitness or promote a label.
- Sentinel STAC metadata provides exact product identities, routes, checksums, sizes, coverage, and catalogue-quality attributes. It does not prove authenticated delivery, archive integrity, local quality, registration, or burn/background truth.
- The source snapshot records five event boundaries and 15 bounded STAC windows. No provider pixel or archive byte was downloaded.
