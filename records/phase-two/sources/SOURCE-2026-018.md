## Role findings

- MTBS boundary and thematic severity are analyst-interpreted remote-sensing products; threshold selection is subjective and ecologically dependent.
- RAVG estimates vegetation condition, is calibrated for forest cover, and masks water, snow, clouds, smoke, shadow, active fire, and other unmappable conditions.
- BAER BARC is preliminary input to BAER/SBS assessment; public availability varies and final soil-burn-severity products are preferred when available.
- Landsat C2 Level-3 Burned Area provides acquisition-based 30 m burn probability and thresholded classification across ecosystems. It has QA, water/shadow, coverage, and commission/omission limitations and remains algorithmic reference evidence.
- Annual NLCD Collection 1.2 provides 30 m annual land-cover context through 2025; land cover is not fire evidence or affirmative background by itself.
- Copernicus Sentinel data remain free, full, and open with no suitability warranty; BurnLens still owes attribution and exact product provenance.
