## Implemented method

- Reverify the exact two-product registered package and accepted optical predecessor.
- Reopen the native B04, B8A, B12, SCL, and true-color AOI windows.
- Derive independent reflectance gradient-magnitude signals for pre and post scenes; use no shared mask in correlation.
- Cover the AOI once with twelve fixed 150 x 150 native-20m windows.
- Estimate post-to-pre row/column translation with a Hann taper, phase-only cross-power spectrum, and localized 100x DFT refinement.
- Preserve per-band peak confidence, median vector consensus, band deviation, pair-quality fractions, window reason codes, and explicit no-label effect.
- Fail closed at 90% eligible coverage, two confident bands, peak ratio 2.0, 0.15-pixel band deviation, and 0.50-pixel residual.
- Render deterministic normalized JSON, semantic HTML, and a 1,800 by 1,250 PNG with full application/model/data trace fields.

## Evidence defect caught before acceptance

The first final JSON audit showed that product report status was present but selected messages and numeric values were empty. In the real Sentinel XML, `<message>` and `<extraValues>` are siblings of `<inspection>` inside `<check>`, while the initial parser searched only inspection descendants. Source commit `5865a59df503f9757c6c62fb40d917e60f210f4a` corrects the bounded parser and updates the fixture to match the real structure. The final report now preserves exact image-refining values plus the explicit “VNIR / SWIR bands have not been registered” and “Spatio Residual Histograms not computed” cautions for both scenes. Registration results did not change.

A final package audit also found that `burnlens.__version__` had remained at `0.6.0` while project metadata advanced. Generator source `5287704a37f03d96e47467afba8623f7be643129` aligns the import-visible version with `0.8.0` and adds a regression check before regenerating the final trace-bearing artifacts.
