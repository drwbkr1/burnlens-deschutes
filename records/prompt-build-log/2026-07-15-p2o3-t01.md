## Packaged quality evidence

The exact pre and post SAFE archives each contain one datastrip `QI_DATA/GEOMETRIC_QUALITY.xml`. Both reports are globally `PASSED` and preserve image-refining, absolute-location, stability, attitude, and ephemeris checks. Both also report that VNIR/SWIR bands have not been registered and that spatio-residual histograms were not computed. The implementation parses only the bounded selected fields and explicitly prevents the product report from satisfying the pair-local gate.

## Implemented method

- Reverify the exact two-product registered package and accepted optical predecessor.
- Reopen the native B04, B8A, B12, SCL, and true-color AOI windows.
- Derive independent reflectance gradient-magnitude signals for pre and post scenes; use no shared mask in correlation.
- Cover the AOI once with twelve fixed 150 x 150 native-20m windows.
- Estimate post-to-pre row/column translation with a Hann taper, phase-only cross-power spectrum, and localized 100x DFT refinement.
- Preserve per-band peak confidence, median vector consensus, band deviation, pair-quality fractions, window reason codes, and explicit no-label effect.
- Fail closed at 90% eligible coverage, two confident bands, peak ratio 2.0, 0.15-pixel band deviation, and 0.50-pixel residual.
- Render deterministic normalized JSON, semantic HTML, and a 1,800 by 1,250 PNG with full application/model/data trace fields.

## Synthetic verification

Fourier-shift tests recover the shift to apply to the moving image within 0.02 pixel for injected shifts `(0.30, -0.40)`, `(-0.17, 0.22)`, `(1.25, -2.10)`, and `(0, 0)` at 0.01-pixel sampling. Textureless signals fail closed. Tests also prove fixed AOI coverage, quality-based exclusion, render determinism, semantic HTML, and that a final visual decision cannot overrule a failed machine gate.
