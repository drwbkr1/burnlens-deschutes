## Acceptance criteria

- Petes Lake can use one event/two decisions without waiting for another fire.
- Normal future batches accept 4-6 candidates; seven or eight require an explicit size exception; nine always fails.
- Candidate order is frozen inside contiguous event groups and every candidate binds the exact proposal, raster, evidence, and visible limitations.
- The surface has no approve-all, prefilled choice, confidence, prior decision, or running vote total.
- Drafts may remain incomplete; completed responses require every candidate decision and one owner attestation.
- Finalization shows a per-candidate summary, exports hash-named exact bytes, and locks the browser session.
- Pre-reveal custody validates bindings without reading decision/note values and writes exact response plus receipt with repository-local ignored no-overwrite semantics.
- Distinct valid finals for one surface are reported as ambiguous rather than selected by timestamp.
- Historical generators and their focused tests remain unchanged and passing.
